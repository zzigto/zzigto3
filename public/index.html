IOT HARDWARE INTERFACE AND SOFTWARE PLATFORM

The interface of any device with an IOT platform is a complex topic that requires a lot of knowledge. zzigto allows the creation of a database-based server architecture to manage connected peripherals, users, sensor data and everything that makes IOT a device. zzigto allows you to create your architecture in a few minutes and use it in a completely personalized way..

The project, a complete platform accessible through a hardware interface, to develop products with great savings of time and money. It is sufficient to integrate the chip with the zzigto firmware in the device with which to establish a serial communication and your product will become immediately controllable from the network, and the data can be exported..

Who needs zzigto

ZZIGTO is an IOT platform designed to provide an interface service to the IOT world for products, prototypes or custom systems. The interface is represented by an ESP32 or ESP8266 processor with zzigto firmware. Network services, web browsing, access to historical data, and definition of triggers are acquired simply by using the interface chip.

Once you have integrated your device with the zzigto platform via the chip, obviously the two things will become one. The zzigto service can be activated by creating an account from your customers, directly from your site. zzigto will provide you with daily consumption data of your customers, and you can use them for your commercial policies, and invoice completely independently. zzigto will have a business relationship only with your company, independently of how you operate with your customers.

Firmware ESP32 Side

Standard zzigto firmware, allows the use of the esp32 or esp8266 chip as an IOT interface to any electronic device that can be interfaced via UART. It allows you to design your own devices and insert them in an zzigto network to make them interact with each other and control them via the internet.

Hardware ESP32 based interface

At the end of the process, the devices must be produced and prepared for distribution. zzigto supports the entire process, from the creation of serial ports to identify each device, to the burning of the selected esp32 chips, up to the generation of labels with serial codes to be affixed to each product produced and destined for the market. The company information system can also be fed with the serial codes of the products generated to complete the warehouse archives.
